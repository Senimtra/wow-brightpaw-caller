# PET-caller

💜 **Brightpaw, always by your side.**

PET-caller is a tiny WoW addon that makes sure your pet **Brightpaw** is summoned whenever you mount your **Mystic Runesaber**.

## What it does
- 🐾 Summons Brightpaw when you mount Mystic Runesaber
- ✅ Keeps it simple and lightweight

## Status
- 🟣 Loaded message: `PET-caller loaded v1.0`

## Notes
- If the pet is already out, nothing changes.
